Autonomous vehicles, automation and robotization

VRE that provides a tool for simulation, evaluation, and monitoring in virtual environments of autonomous vehicles, experimenting with different detection and actuation technologies with the goal of understanding and verifying the robot's operation in dynamic scenarios.

Near and remote sensing of agriculture: Sensor integration and connectivity

VRE that allows access to information in almost real time, as well as in historical series, to the different sources of information offered by the Sentinel constellations for an area of interest (AOI) defined by the user: RADAR images (Sentinel 1), optical images (Sentinel 2) and emission and concentration of gases in the atmosphere (Sentinel 5).

Open twins

Innovative open-source platform that specializes in developing next-gen compositional digital twins.

Information management, simulators, demonstrations, serious games and digital twins

VRE that provides a tool linked to the digital twin developed for the irrigation machine (PIVOT), including the 3D model, visualization system of the machine movement in the plot, and area of action, access to information of the machine components (hydraulic elements and sensors), access to the generated outputs: irrigation charts and maps of humidity, yield or vegetation index.
