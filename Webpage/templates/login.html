Smartfood-GEE portal

Welcome to SmartFood-GEE! The SmartFood project provides robust services for the identification of the factors involved in the impact on biodiversity and ecosystems, agriculture, livestock production and fisheries.

The SmartFood Virtual Research Environment services include Open Data repositories and tools to capture scientific data and data analytics solutions. The data analytics solutions can combine public data with private data that will be kept protected in your own user profile.
