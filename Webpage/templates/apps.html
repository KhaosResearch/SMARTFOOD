Sentinel 1

Sentinel-1 offers radar images that can penetrate through clouds and darkness, making them valuable for Earth surface monitoring applications such as detecting ground changes, tracking displacements, monitoring changes in vegetation cover, and managing natural disasters.

Sentinel 2

Sentinel-2 provides high-resolution optical images that capture information in multiple electromagnetic bands. These images are used for monitoring vegetation, agriculture, forest management, urban planning, and detecting changes on the Earth's surface.

SigPac S2 Column S2

Sentinel 5P

Sentinel-5P is designed to measure the composition of the Earth's atmosphere, providing information on the concentration of gases such as sulfur dioxide, nitrogen dioxide, carbon monoxide, ozone, and other atmospheric pollutants. These data are crucial for air quality monitoring and tracking atmospheric pollution.
